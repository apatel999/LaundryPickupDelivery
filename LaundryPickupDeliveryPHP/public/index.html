Frequently Asked Questions

How does the pickup and delivery work?
We offer flexible pickup and delivery time slots. Simply book online, and our team will come to your door at the scheduled time. We'll handle everything from there!
What if I have special care instructions?
You can add notes during booking with any special care instructions. Our team is trained to handle delicate fabrics and specific requirements.
How long does the service take?
Most orders are completed within 24-48 hours, depending on your selected time slots and current volume. Rush service is available for an additional fee.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept cash payment upon delivery. Payment is collected securely when your clean laundry is delivered to you.
What if something is damaged, and do you sort laundry by color?
We handle all laundry with care and attention. In the unlikely event of any issue, please contact us right away and we will work with you to resolve it as quickly as possible. Please note, we do not separate white and colored garments—customers must ensure items are properly sorted prior to service. We are not responsible for any damage, staining, or color transfer resulting from unsorted laundry.
